# Search Strategy

**Project**: HIV PrEP Network Meta-Analysis
**Date**: 2026-03-28

---

## PubMed Search String

```
(("Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis"[MeSH] OR "PrEP"[tiab] OR "pre-exposure prophylaxis"[tiab])
AND
("HIV Infections"[MeSH:noexp] OR "HIV-1"[tiab] OR "HIV prevention"[tiab])
AND
(
  "tenofovir"[tiab] OR "emtricitabine"[tiab] OR "TDF/FTC"[tiab] OR "Truvada"[tiab]
  OR "TAF/FTC"[tiab] OR "Descovy"[tiab] OR "tenofovir alafenamide"[tiab]
  OR "cabotegravir"[tiab] OR "CAB-LA"[tiab] OR "Apretude"[tiab]
  OR "lenacapavir"[tiab] OR "GS-6207"[tiab] OR "PURPOSE"[tiab]
  OR "dapivirine"[tiab] OR "vaginal ring"[tiab] OR "intravaginal ring"[tiab]
)
AND
("randomized controlled trial"[pt] OR "randomised controlled trial"[tiab]
 OR "RCT"[tiab] OR "clinical trial, phase III"[pt] OR "clinical trial, phase II"[pt]))
```

Filters: 2000/01/01 – 2026/03/28; All languages

---

## Embase Search String

```
('HIV pre-exposure prophylaxis'/exp OR 'preexposure prophylaxis':ab,ti OR 'PrEP':ab,ti)
AND
('HIV infection'/exp OR 'HIV-1':ab,ti)
AND
('tenofovir':ab,ti OR 'emtricitabine':ab,ti OR 'cabotegravir':ab,ti
 OR 'lenacapavir':ab,ti OR 'dapivirine':ab,ti OR 'vaginal ring':ab,ti)
AND
('randomized controlled trial'/exp OR 'randomization':ab,ti OR 'RCT':ab,ti)
```

---

## Cochrane CENTRAL

- Search within Cochrane CENTRAL register of controlled trials
- Terms: "HIV prevention" AND ("PrEP" OR "pre-exposure prophylaxis") AND
  ("tenofovir" OR "cabotegravir" OR "lenacapavir" OR "dapivirine")

---

## ClinicalTrials.gov

- Condition: "HIV infections"
- Intervention: "PrEP" OR "pre-exposure prophylaxis"
- Status: Completed, Terminated
- Study type: Interventional (clinical trial)
- Phase: 2, 3

---

## Hand-search / Grey Literature

- CROI conference abstracts 2010–2026
- IAS (International AIDS Society) conference abstracts 2010–2026
- AIDS 2024 conference abstracts (Munich)
- HIVR4P 2024 conference (Lima)
- References of included trials and existing systematic reviews
