**Abbreviations:** IRR = incidence rate ratio; SUCRA = Surface Under the Cumulative Ranking curve

**† Regulatory status as of March 2026:**
- *Lenacapavir*: FDA-approved June 2024 for PrEP; EMA conditional approval; not yet available in most LMICs.
- *Cabotegravir LA*: FDA-approved December 2021; EMA-approved 2023; regulatory approvals expanding in sub-Saharan Africa.
- *Dapivirine vaginal ring*: WHO-prequalified 2020; approved in several sub-Saharan African countries and EU (Article 58); not FDA-approved.
